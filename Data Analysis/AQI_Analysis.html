Summary

Query 1 - Least and most polluted city in 2016


In this query, I am comparing the AQI values of Boston, New York City, Chicago, San Francisco and Los Angeles for the year 2016. It is evident that Boston has the best AQI while Los Angeles is very polluted .

Query 2 - Best Time to visit New York


Here, I am trying to find the best time to visit New York City by finding the average AQI of each month for the past 6 years. From the figure, it is clear that the months April and October have low AQI making them good months while January, July and December have very high AQI values.

Query 3 - Correlation of AQI with latitude


Here, I am trying to find if there is any correlation between air quality and latitude. So, I plot the air quality index of each latitude averaged across all the months for the year 2015. The plot shows comparatively low AQI values for lower latitudes - it kinda makes sense since the southern part of USA is mostly desert. It also shows very high AQI for higher latitude - Alaska seems to have higher AQI values (which is validated in the next query as well).

Query 4 - Comparison of AQI values in 1999 and 2015


Here, I am trying to find how the AQI of states has changed from 1999 to 2015. I do this by taking the average AQI of each state in both the years and finding the difference. I plot the difference on the map. Any state with positive value means that the AQI values have decreased from 1999 to 2015 while the states with negative increase have improved their AQI.

From the figure, it is evident that California, Alabama, Georgia have had a significant increase in quality of air while Alaska, Idaho, Montana, Texas have worsened air quality.

Machine Learning Model 1 & 2 - Linear Regression


Here, I used linear regression to find if I can find any relation between latitude and aqi values and longitude and aqi values. I used the data I got for Query 3. On visualizing, it was clear that linear regression is not at all able to model the problem correctly.

Machine Learning Model 3 - Decision Trees


I try to find some relation between longitude, latitude and the AQI values, using the data I got from Query 3. Instead of using the AQI values themselves, I encoded the values depending on the guidelines given in the EPA site . The decision tree visualization gives more insight than what I obtained from regression.
